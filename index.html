Frequently Asked Questions

Is interstate tax included in the fare?

For one-way trips, interstate taxes are usually included. For round trips, you may need to pay state taxes directly at the border checkposts. Please check the fare breakdown.

Can I change my booking date?

Yes, you can modify your booking date up to 24 hours before the trip free of charge via our app or website.

Are pets allowed in the cab?

Small pets are allowed in specific vehicles. Please inform customer support immediately after booking so we can arrange a pet-friendly car.
